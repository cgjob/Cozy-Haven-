Quick 15-Minute Creamy Garlic Pasta

Busy weeknights don't mean you have to settle for frozen, tasteless meals. This 15-minute creamy garlic pasta requires only 5 key pantry staples but delivers a premium, restaurant-quality meal.

The Quick Ingredient List:

1. Pasta of your choice (Spaghetti or Penne works beautifully)
2. Fresh garlic (4 cloves, minced)
3. Heavy cream (or coconut cream for a dairy-free version)
4. Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
5. Extra virgin olive oil, salt, and freshly cracked black pepper

Step-by-Step Method:

While boiling your pasta in salted water, heat olive oil in a wide pan over low heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté gently for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for another 2 minutes. Right before the pasta is cooked, scoop out half a cup of pasta water. Drain the pasta and toss it directly into the creamy garlic pan with the grated Parmesan cheese and the splash of pasta water. Toss on low heat until glossy.
