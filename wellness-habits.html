5 Simple Morning Habits for Less Stress

How you spend the first hour of your morning sets the baseline for your mental health and stress tolerance for the rest of the day. By building simple, intentional wellness habits, you can stop rushing and start living.

1. The 30-Minute Phone-Free Window

Do not check your emails, social media, or news notifications immediately after waking up. Flooding your brain with external information first thing triggers instant stress hormones. Give yourself 30 quiet minutes.

2. Hydrate Before You Caffeine

Drink a large glass of warm water before reaching for coffee. Dehydration mimics anxiety symptoms. Rehydrating first resets your body's nervous system.

3. Simple Box Breathing

Before jumping out of bed, spend 3 minutes practicing box breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4, and hold for 4. It instantly lowers your heart rate.
